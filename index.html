About

Jim is a theatremaker and facilitator based in Dublin, Ireland. He is a graduate of Trinity College Dublin. Jim writes plays, performs, and leads workshops.

Theatre

Jim utilises mixed media, archive material, and academic research to create layered theatrical texts. His hope is to make work that feels expansive, engaging, and unexpected.

Facilitation

Jim is a volunteer with Fighting Words Dublin, where he facilitates one-off creative writing workshops as well as participating in longer projects.
